Privacy Policy - Contacts Export

Effective date: 2026-06-29 · Contact: damaerguo@gmail.com

Overview

Contacts Export ("the App") is a contacts backup and export tool that helps you export your address book into CSV, Excel, and VCF files for backup, cross-platform migration, and spreadsheet/CRM use. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Important: What Contacts Export Does

The App does NOT upload your contacts to our servers. All data processing happens locally on your device. The App reads your contacts only with your explicit permission, generates export files in memory, and never transmits contact content over the internet.

Data the App accesses

With your permission, the App accesses the following:

1. System Contacts (Read Access)

  • The App reads your contacts within the scope granted by the system to build export files
  • The App does not create, modify, or delete any contacts
  • If the system returns iOS limited permission, the App only exports contacts within that limited scope
  • All contact data remains on your device and in your system/cloud address book

2. File Access

  • The App writes exported files (CSV, Excel, VCF) to a temporary cache directory on your device
  • The App uses the system Share Sheet when you choose to share, save, or send an exported file
  • Cached export files are automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • File actions are initiated only by your explicit action

3. App Settings and Preferences

  • Language preference (English, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, Japanese)
  • Export format and field selection (16 optional fields)
  • Purchase/entitlement status (verified by App Store)

The App does NOT require an account, registration, or login.

How the App uses data

  • Export: The App reads selected contacts and generates files in your chosen format (CSV, Excel, VCF) entirely on your device
  • Field selection: You choose which of the 16 fields to include. Field selection affects file contents only, never the number of contacts
  • Share: The App hands the generated file to the system Share Sheet. Where you send it (email, cloud, another app) is your choice
  • Restore Purchase: The App verifies entitlement with the App Store so your one-time purchase is restored on new devices

Data sharing and transfers

  • The App does NOT sell your data
  • The App does NOT share your data with third parties for advertising
  • The App does NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII)
  • The App does NOT use any analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs

No contact content, file contents, or personal information is ever transmitted off your device. The only external communication is with the Apple App Store for purchase verification and entitlement restore, which does not involve your contact data.

Data storage and deletion

  • Contacts: Stored in your device's system address book and your cloud accounts (iCloud, Google, etc.). The App never copies or stores a separate contacts database. Manage or delete them via your system Contacts app.
  • Exported files: Written to a temporary cache directory. Automatically deleted after 24 hours, or sooner if you clear the App cache or uninstall.
  • App settings: Field selections and preferences are stored locally on your device. Clear via the App or uninstall.

Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Contacts (Read) — to read contacts for building export files
  • File / Storage Access — to write exported files to a temporary cache and hand them to the system Share Sheet

If you deny contacts permission, the export feature will not function. The App will not be able to read or export any contacts.

Purchase and entitlement

  • The App uses a one-time purchase (buyout) model — pay once, use forever. There is no subscription and no auto-renewal.
  • Free version limitation: Exports the first 30 contacts (selected deterministically by system order; not randomized, stable across sessions)
  • Paid version: Exports all contacts within the granted permission scope
  • Payment is processed by Apple App Store
  • The App does NOT receive or store your payment card details
  • Your purchase can be restored on a new device via Restore Purchase

Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — For purchase processing and entitlement verification
  • System Share Sheet — For sharing exported files (handled entirely by the OS)

The App does NOT use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, social tracking, or any third-party data collection.

Data security

  • All contact data processing happens locally on your device
  • No contact content is transmitted over the internet, except App Store purchase verification (which does not involve contact data)
  • Exported files live only in a temporary cache and expire automatically
  • The App follows platform security best practices for contacts access

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your rights

  • Access: You can view all your contacts in your system Contacts app at any time
  • Deletion: The App stores no separate copy of your contacts. Exported files are auto-deleted from cache after 24 hours
  • Opt-out: You can uninstall the App at any time. Your contacts remain in your address book, untouched by the App
  • Purchase management: Your one-time purchase is managed via App Store settings and can be restored on new devices

Compliance

  • GDPR: The App processes contact data locally and does not transfer personal data to third parties
  • CCPA: The App does not sell personal information
