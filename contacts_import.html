Privacy Policy - Contacts Import

Effective date: 2026-06-29 · Contact: damaerguo@gmail.com

Overview

Contacts Import ("the App") is a contacts import tool that helps you import contacts into your address book from CSV and VCF files — for restoring backups, migrating between devices, or adding bulk contacts. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Important: What Contacts Import Does

The App does NOT upload your contacts or files to our servers. All parsing, field mapping, and writing happens locally on your device. The App reads your selected files and writes to your address book only with your explicit permission and final confirmation.

Data the App accesses

With your permission, the App accesses the following:

1. System Contacts (Write Access)

  • The App creates new contacts in your address book when you confirm an import
  • The App does not modify or delete existing contacts — it only adds
  • Import runs only after you review the count, skipped duplicates, and target account, and explicitly confirm
  • All imported contacts are stored in your device's system address book and your cloud accounts

2. File Access

  • The App reads CSV and VCF files that you select for import
  • Files are parsed in memory. The App does not copy your import file into its own storage or keep a permanent copy
  • File access is initiated only by your explicit action

3. Notifications

  • The App may show a local notification when a large import finishes in the background
  • Notifications are delivered locally by the system and contain no contact data

4. App Settings and Preferences

  • Language preference (English, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, Japanese)
  • Field mapping and import configuration
  • Purchase/entitlement status (verified by App Store)

The App does NOT require an account, registration, or login.

How the App uses data

  • Parse: The App reads your selected CSV/VCF file and extracts records entirely on your device
  • Map & preview: The App matches file columns to contact fields and shows a preview before anything is written. Nothing is written until you confirm
  • Import: The App creates contacts in your address book at your request, skipping duplicates as configured
  • Restore Purchase: The App verifies entitlement with the App Store so your one-time purchase is restored on new devices

Data sharing and transfers

  • The App does NOT sell your data
  • The App does NOT share your data with third parties for advertising
  • The App does NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII)
  • The App does NOT use any analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs

No contact content, file contents, or personal information is ever transmitted off your device. The only external communication is with the Apple App Store for purchase verification and entitlement restore, which does not involve your contact or file data.

Data storage and deletion

  • Imported files: Processed in memory and not stored by the App. Original files remain wherever you saved them.
  • Imported contacts: Stored in your device's system address book and your cloud accounts (iCloud, Google, etc.). Delete using your system Contacts app.
  • App settings: Field mappings and preferences are stored locally on your device. Clear via the App or uninstall.

Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Contacts (Add) — to write imported contacts into your address book
  • File Access — to read the CSV/VCF files you select for import
  • Notifications — to notify you when a background import completes (optional)

If you deny contacts permission, the import feature will not function. The App cannot create contacts without it.

Purchase and entitlement

  • The App uses a one-time purchase (buyout) model — pay once, use forever. There is no subscription and no auto-renewal.
  • Free version: Lets you parse, map, and preview an import to evaluate the result before paying
  • Paid version: Unlocks full import — safely writes the complete batch of contacts into your address book
  • Payment is processed by Apple App Store
  • The App does NOT receive or store your payment card details
  • Your purchase can be restored on a new device via Restore Purchase

Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — For purchase processing and entitlement verification

The App does NOT use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, social tracking, or any third-party data collection.

Data security

  • All file parsing and contact writing happens locally on your device
  • No contact content or file contents are transmitted over the internet, except App Store purchase verification (which does not involve your data)
  • The App never creates, modifies, or deletes contacts before your explicit confirmation
  • The App follows platform security best practices for contacts access

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your rights

  • Access: You can view all your contacts, including imported ones, in your system Contacts app at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete imported contacts via your system Contacts app. The App keeps no separate copy
  • Opt-out: You can uninstall the App at any time. Imported contacts remain in your address book unless you delete them
  • Purchase management: Your one-time purchase is managed via App Store settings and can be restored on new devices

Compliance

  • GDPR: The App processes contact and file data locally and does not transfer personal data to third parties
  • CCPA: The App does not sell personal information
